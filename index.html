How It Works

Three steps to unlock your potential

Step 01

Open the App

Open the app and input your training goal for today. Your AI coach analyzes your history and sets a personalized session plan.

Step 02

Start Your Physical Workout

Begin your workout. The app tracks your physical output in real time — heart rate zones, reps, exertion, and recovery windows.

Step 03

Talk to Your AI Coach

While you train, speak to your AI coach. It delivers real-time cognitive exercises timed to your physical state — maximizing neuroplasticity when your brain is most receptive.
